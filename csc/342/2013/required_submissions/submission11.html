Overview

This week your task is to put some regular expressions to work analyzing and reformatting a textfile of input data. The input textfile must contain two lines of data for each of an arbitray number of individuals. The two lines for any given individual look like this:

Lastname:Firstname:A00123456:csc34201
(902)420-5790

The first of these two lines, in each case, may be assumed to be correct, and to have the format shown above. The only thing you need to note here is that Lastname may contain a blank space, a dash, or an apostrophe (single quote), while Firstname consists only of letters.

The second line, in each case, contains a (North American style) phone number, with no leading or trailing spaces. In other words, there must be nothing on the second line but the phone number. However, this phone number may or may not be a "valid" phone number. To be "valid", in our context, a phone number must satisfy the following criteria:

  1. It must have a 3-digit area code, a 3-digit exchange, and a 4-digit number. We refer to these as the "three parts" of the phone number.
  2. The three parts of the phone number may be separated by nothing, by a space, by a dash, or by a period. When there are no parenthese around the area code, whatever (if anything) separates the first and second part must also separate the second and third parts. That is, for example, you cannot have a dash separating the area code and exchange, with a period separating the exchange and the number in that exchange.
  3. The area code may, or may not be enclosed in parentheses. That is, if one of the parentheses is present, the other must be present as well. If the area code is enclosed in parentheses, the exchange either begins immediately after the right parenthesis, or is separated from it by a single blank space, and the exchange and number may then be separated by nothing, by a space, by a dash, or by a period.

The three lines of output corresponging to the above two lines of input, when the phone number is valid, look like this:

Name .......... Firstname Lastname
                SMU ID: A00123456   Account: csc34201
                (902)405-2585       Area code: 902  Exchange: 405  Number: 2585

When the phone number is not valid, the three lines of output corresponding to the above two lines of input look like this:

Name .......... Firstname Lastname
                SMU ID: A00123456  Account: csc34201
                Invalid number: (902)405!2585

The information output for each eight individuals (24 lines) occupies one standard screen, with room for a pause at the bottom.
