Use of Blackboard in CSCI 2342

Blackboard is a course delivery system that we will employ mainly for making the marks you receive in the course available on-line. It has other facilities which we may utilize if there is a need or a demand for them.

To access Blackboard you need to go to SMUport link on the SMU home page, log in with your "s number", and click on the "My Courses" link. This should take you to a page listing the courses you are taking, and if you then click on the link for CSCI 2342 you should be able to access your marks, as well as any other facilities that have been made available.
